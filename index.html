    Instructions

    You are invited to take part in a study by Utrecht University on how people plan. Participation is voluntary, and you may ask questions at any time.

    • In this study, you will play several rounds of a Bin Packing game. In each round, you will see different items in a resource area and must move them into a smaller target area so that everything fits. Each puzzle has a solution using one bin area, but you may use a second area if needed.

    • The puzzles will vary in difficulty and time limits. You may take a short break after doing 10 trials. You will also be asked to complete a brief questionnaire about your demographics, and background and experience with the task.

    • During the task, we will do a screen recording and your actions will be logged for research purposes.

    • The experiment will take up to 1 hour.

    • The experiment is completely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without penalty.

          About this game

          This is a 2D geometric bin-packing game. You have to pack all items from the left side into the minimum number of bins on the right side.
